Project

Skgchess

Status: Live on the web

1. Overview

SKG Chess is a multiplayer-only chess platform featuring:

  • Historic photos of the city of Thessaloniki
  • Custom chess engine
  • Full game history tracking
  • All popular time controls (Rapid, Blitz, Classical)
  • Real-time invitation system
  • Integrated in-game chat
  • Classical music in the background
  • Inspirational chess quotes
  • Three selectable board themes

It is available in both Greek and English.

2. Background

In November 2024, I started creating a chess game in Java by following the video tutorials of Software Architecture & Design on YouTube, as a way to expand my knowledge in my work as a software engineer.

Along the way, the project evolved into something much bigger and more personal, driven by my love for chess and my need for creative expression.

3. Release

In October 2025, I completed SKG Chess and released it publicly.
