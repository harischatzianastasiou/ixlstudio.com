GET TO KNOW ME

My name is Haris Chatzianastasiou, and I am a software developer living in Thessaloniki, Greece. For the last 3 years I have been working in the IT industry and have been involved in large projects in the private and public sector. I am a holder of a Bachelor’s degree in Computer Science with Biomedical Informatics, and recently I began my Master’s degree in Artificial Intelligence.

EVERY PROJECT IS A UNIQUE STORY

I believe that software, similar to any service, exists to address specific needs and achieve its intended purpose. For this, several factors must be considered. Data-driven products must work reliably, securely and be easily scalable to support future growth. The content that users see should be structured in a way that conveys the product's importance, expresses the brand's identity, and engages the user. Navigation flow should be intuitive, as a negative user experience can significantly lead to lost conversions and damage to brand reputation.

The tasks described span the core concepts of software creation—backend and frontend development, along with UX design. As a fullstack developer, I view my work as a way to craft unique stories by connecting these disciplines. Every finished product tells a story. Whether working on a large corporate project, bringing a brand's vision to life, or helping a local business build an online presence, at the center of each project are people ready to tell a story to the world, and I am more than happy to help them.
