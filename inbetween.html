Project

In Between

← Back to Projects

Project In Between is my personal attempt to create a game that evokes emotion. It exists for a few reasons.

First, it's a way to explore meaning and express myself.

Second, it’s about trying to make a genuinely good game. Learning the disciplines of game design, sound, art, storytelling, and world-building within the limits of what a single person can create.

Finally, it's about sharing the process publicly, starting from where I am now — at the very beginning.

My goal is to document my progress so there will be a transparent record of how someone starts from the early formation of an idea and moves, step by step, toward producing something that fulfills its intent.

At the same time, I want to connect more deeply with people through something I create and feels authentic to me.

If you are interested in following the project, stay tuned in ixlstudio.com or subscribe to my YouTube channel.
