0. Introduction

This is a project about expressing myself through creating a game. I am starting from scratch and will document the entire process, in case it helps someone else who is interested.

2. Sharing the process

Sharing the process online fulfills the original intent of this page as a public experiment in creation. If an audience forms, that presence will inevitably interact with the process itself. Feedback, interpretation, and observation become part of the environment in in which the game is being made. In that sense, the audience is not separate from the work; it influences it.

At the same time, it enables me to connect with people by creating something authentic to me, without waiting for it to be finished.

Lastly, documenting the progress of making the game (including the internal dialogue and the decisions made) creates a transparent record of how an idea begins in its earliest form and moves, step by step, toward becoming something that fulfills its intent. This can be useful for others who are starting their own journey.
